# 📊 Business Insights 360

An end-to-end **Business Intelligence** solution built with **Power BI** to transform raw business data into interactive dashboards and actionable insights. This project provides a 360° view of business performance across **Finance, Sales, Marketing, Supply Chain, and Executive Management**, enabling data-driven decision-making.

# 📌 Project Overview

Business Insights 360 is an end-to-end Business Intelligence project that consolidates data from multiple business functions into one centralized reporting solution.

The dashboard empowers executives and business users to:

* Monitor company-wide KPIs
* Analyze financial performance
* Track sales growth
* Measure marketing effectiveness
* Improve supply chain efficiency
* Make informed business decisions using real-time insights

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

* Monitor overall business performance
* Analyze financial health and profitability
* Evaluate sales performance across markets and products
* Measure marketing effectiveness
* Improve supply chain efficiency
* Deliver executive-level KPIs for strategic decision-making
